A Signature Approach

The Ravitz Way™

After years in classrooms (and a few too many late-night study sessions), I developed my own teaching philosophy. I call it The Ravitz Way — and yes, it's trademarked in my heart.

01

Snacks First

A hungry brain learns nothing. Goldfish crackers are non-negotiable.

02

Make It Click

Every kid has a "lightbulb angle." My job is to find it — even if it means teaching long division through Taylor Swift lyrics.

03

Celebrate Wins

Big or small, every breakthrough gets a high-five. Confidence is the real curriculum.
